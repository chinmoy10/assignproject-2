Run an Extra
Mile Easily

To run an extra mile easily, begin with a proper warm-up to prepare your muscles.
Maintain a slower pace to conserve energy and focus on steady breathing to avoid fatigue.
Mentally break the distance into smaller sections to make it feel more manageable.

BMI Calculator

A BMI (Body Mass Index) calculator is a useful tool for fitness purposes,
helping individuals understand their general body weight in relation to their height.

BMI Weight Status

BMI provides an easy method to assess whether you're underweight, normal weight, overweight, or obese.
